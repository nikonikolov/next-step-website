About

Next Step is a volunteer non-profit project aiming to help high-school students interested in tech in their personal aspirations. The project was initiated and is currently maintained by former Bulgarian medalists who won distinctions at International Science Olympiads and Fairs. Our aim is to motivate young Bulgarian students to develop excellence in the fields of tech and exact sciences by providing them useful study resources and helping them gain accomplishments in prestigeous International Science and Engineering Fairs. Moreover, we strive to provide easy access to the "best" steps to take to apply to US universities, where the students can flourish and advance their knowledge.
